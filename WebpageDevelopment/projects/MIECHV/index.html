OUHSC Independent Evaluation of the OK State Competitive MIECHV EBHV Project

In partnership with Oklahoma State Department of Health, OU Health Sciences Center is evaluating Evidence-Based Home Visiting Services. We are giving parents the opportunity to voice concerns and impact services for families throughout our state.

This study involves focus groups of parents that have participated in certain home visiting programs as well as a community survey. We hope that what we learn can make improvements to services.

You may be able to help! We are looking for parents to participate in our research study. Participation consists of a computer-assisted interview. A data collector would schedule a time that is convenient to you to bring a computer to your home. You simply answer a survey that asks you questions about your family's experiences and your thoughts.  It takes about 2 hours, and you will be compensated for your time!


 Eligible participants:
  • Must either be pregnant OR be the primary caregiver for at least one child under 36 months
  • Must be receiving services such as WIC, Medicaid, or home visiting programs
  • Must reside in one of the four selected counties: Oklahoma, Comanche, Muskogee, Tulsa
