OUHSC Section of Developmental and Behavioral Pediatrics

Data Collection Team

Several of our research studies in the Section of Developmental and Behavioral Pediatrics involve data collection in the homes of families. Some of these studies include Giving Parents a Voice and the parentPRO Pilot project. The projects our team works on are intended to improve services for families with young children across Oklahoma.
