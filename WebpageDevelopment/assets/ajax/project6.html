Reproducible Research

Reproducible research is a mentality and technique that makes an investigation transparent for outsiders to inspect and potentially conduct on their own. Here are some code & report repositories of studies conducted by BBMC members. Many more are in the pipeline, to be released in 2015 & 2016.
