parentPRO Pilot Evaluation Project
OUHSC Department of Pediatrics are conducting the external evaluation of the parentPRO Pilot. parentPRO Pilot is a new method of service delivery Oklahoma State Department of Health is rolling out in four selected Oklahoma counties for a more streamlined and customized delivery of Evidence-Based Home Visiting. parentPRO Pilot is using the Parents As Teachers (PAT) model with the new additions of case managers, community connectors, and tailored levels of services for the varied needs of families. The research team will be evaluating outcomes for parentPRO Pilot in comparison with families receiving services as usual and those receiving no services. Cost-effectiveness will also be evaluated as compared to services as usual, and the process implementation are being monitored using semi-structured one-on-one interviews with OSDH staff.
